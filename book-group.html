Group sessions

Group sessions with Kind Soul Yoga are a great way to improve your practice and connect with other yogis. Due to COVID-19, all classes are currently online and take place over Zoom - if you are interested in joining us for a group session please sign up to the KSY mailing list to receive the most up to date Zoom meeting links.
Please join each class 5 minutes before the start time and make sure you have a bottle of water to stay hydrated. Wear loose, comfortable clothing and make sure the area around you is clear.

So come along and let's practice, improve and learn!
